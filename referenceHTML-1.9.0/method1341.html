Python Method PostulateHigherOrderRotationAxes

Owner: Class PointGroupFinder

Analyze group connectivity by distance to determine possible proper and improper rotation axes.

Only axes of order >= 3 are searched for. In addition, composite symmetry operations are omitted. Thus, for example all S(2n+1) can be skipped as they automatically imply a C(2n+1) axis with a perpendicular mirror plane.
